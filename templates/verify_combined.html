Combined Face and Signature Verification

1
Face Verification

Face Guidelines:

  • Make sure only one person appears in the camera.
  • Ensure proper lighting and a clear background.
  • Remove hats, sunglasses or other face coverings.
2
Signature Verification

Signature Guidelines:

  • Ensure the signature is clearly visible against a contrasting background.
  • The signature should be centered in the image.
  • Use black ink on white paper for best results.
